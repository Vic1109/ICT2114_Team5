**Long-Term Recommendations:**

2.  **Strengthen Authentication**: For services exposed to the internet (e.g., RDP, Telnet, SMTP), enforce strong, complex passwords, multi-factor authentication (MFA), and implement account lockout policies to thwart brute-force attacks.

4.  **Regular Vulnerability Scanning**: Conduct regular external and internal vulnerability scans and penetration tests to identify and remediate weaknesses proactively.

6.  **Threat Intelligence Integration**: Integrate threat intelligence feeds to automatically block known malicious IPs and URLs.
7.  **Security Awareness Training**: Educate staff on phishing and social engineering techniques, as initial access often begins with user-level compromise.
